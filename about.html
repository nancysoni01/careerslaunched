About

CareersLaunched is for students figuring out what comes next.

Choosing a direction can feel abstract when most students have only seen a small slice of what work actually looks like. This site makes career exploration more concrete through interviews, resources, and honest stories from different fields.

The goal is not to pressure anyone into having a perfect plan. It is to help students ask better questions and notice paths they may not have known existed.

Founder note

Kaia Soni

I started CareersLaunched after a big transition in my own life. I was a figure skater doing online school, and after an injury, I returned to regular school during sophomore year. Around that time, I noticed how many students, especially in online school, felt unsure about what they wanted to do next. I felt that uncertainty too.

CareersLaunched is here to help high school and college students explore different careers, understand their options, and feel more prepared for the future, especially as AI continues to change the way people learn, work, and plan their next steps.
